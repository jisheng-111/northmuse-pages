Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Northmuse respects your privacy. This app is designed as a local utility for heading, coordinates, and saved points, with no account system and no cloud sync.

Information We Collect

Northmuse does not collect personal information for account creation, login, or cloud services.

The app may access the following on your device:

  • Location data to show heading, direction, coordinates, altitude, and accuracy.
  • Saved points that you choose to create inside the app.

How Location Data Is Used

Location access is used only to provide the app’s core functionality, including:

  • Displaying your current heading and direction
  • Displaying latitude and longitude
  • Displaying altitude and accuracy when available
  • Allowing you to save location points locally

Data Storage

Saved points are stored locally on your device. Northmuse does not provide cloud backup, cloud sync, or account-based storage.

Tracking and Analytics

Northmuse does not track users and does not use third-party analytics for behavior profiling.

Third-Party Sharing

Northmuse does not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Your Control

You can revoke location access at any time in your device Settings. You may also delete saved points directly inside the app.
